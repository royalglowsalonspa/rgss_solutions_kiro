PWA Install Prompt Strategy
  • Trigger: 2nd visit (localStorage visit_count ≥ 2)
  • AND: beforeinstallprompt event captured
  • AND: Not dismissed before
  • AND: Not already installed (display-mode check)
  • "Not Now": Don't show for 7 days
  • Dismissed 3x: Never show again

Header & Footer Specs
  • Header: Sticky, white bg with shadow on scroll, Book Now always visible
  • Mobile menu: Slide from right (250ms ease-out), links + auth state
  • Footer: Dark background, white text, social icons (32px hover → gold)
  • NAP: Must match Google My Business EXACTLY (SEO critical)
  • Cookie Preferences: Link in footer opens consent manager
SPA Duration Toggle
  • All SPA services have 60/90 min toggle (NOT two separate cards)
  • Salon services have fixed duration (no toggle)
  • Toggle stored as selected_duration in booking
  • Price switches dynamically on toggle (no page reload)
  • "Book This" opens booking dialog with service + duration pre-selected
