5. Leave State Machine

Staff submits leave request
PENDING ← Initial state
APPROVED
(by receptionist/manager)
REJECTED
(with reason, staff notified)
WITHDRAWN
(by staff before decision)

Transitions:

  • pending → approved: Receptionist/Manager approves
  • pending → rejected: Receptionist/Manager rejects (reason required)
  • pending → withdrawn: Staff cancels own request before decision

Post-Approval:

  • Schedule grid blocked for those dates
  • Conflicting bookings flagged for reassignment
  • Ably: staff.marked_off published (schedule updates live)

Cannot:

  • Approve/reject after withdrawn
  • Withdraw after approved (must cancel leave separately — manager only)
  • Staff submit overlapping leave dates with existing approved leave

7. Schedule Conflict Handling

CONFLICT: Leave Approved → Existing Confirmed Bookings

Scenario: Meera has confirmed bookings on 21 May, but her sick leave for 21–22 May is approved.

Leave approved
Scan bookings for staff on leave dates
Confirmed bookings found?
Meera, 21 May, 11:00 — Aisha K.
⚠️ Booking Conflict Alert
Action required: Reassign or Cancel

⚠️ Booking Conflict Alert

Meera's leave was approved but she has:
• 21 May 11:00 — Aisha K. (Swedish Massage)

Action required:

Reassign to: Cancel Booking

Resolution options:

  • REASSIGN: Pick another available staff → Customer push: "Stylist changed to {name}" → Booking stays confirmed
  • CANCEL: Cancel the booking → Customer push + email with "Book Again" CTA → booking.status → 'cancelled' → cancel_reason: 'staff_unavailable'

This alert shows immediately after approval (modal on same page). Also appears as persistent banner on /admin/schedule. Cannot be dismissed until all conflicts resolved.

9. Ably Realtime Updates on Schedule

Realtime Schedule Events (Ably channels)

Channel: admin:schedule
Subscribers: All admin users viewing /admin/schedule

EventTriggerUI Effect
slot.bookedBooking confirmed (staff assigned to slot)Slot fills with customer name + colour (animated)
slot.releasedBooking cancelled or rescheduled awaySlot returns to "available" (fade out)
staff.marked_offLeave approved or same-day mark-offFull row turns to LEAVE state (dark overlay slides in)
leave.approvedLeave request approvedFuture date rows pre-marked (if in current view range)
slot.reassignedBooking moved to different staffOld slot releases, new slot fills (simultaneous)

Payload example (slot.booked):

{
  "event": "slot.booked",
  "staffId": "staff_meera_001",
  "date": "2026-05-24",
  "startTime": "11:00",
  "endTime": "12:30",
  "customerName": "Aisha K.",
  "service": "Swedish Massage",
  "bookingId": "bk_abc123"
}

Animation spec:

  • slot.booked: empty cell → colour fill slides from left (200ms)
  • slot.released: colour fades out + shrink (150ms)
  • staff.marked_off: dark overlay slides down row (300ms)
  • slot.reassigned: cross-fade between old/new staff rows (250ms)
