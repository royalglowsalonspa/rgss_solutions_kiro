🔄 Pipeline Steps

  1. Mark booking "Completed" (checkout panel)
  2. Apply offer (optional, max 1 per customer/day)
  3. Select payment method (Cash / UPI / Card)
  4. Generate invoice row with line-item snapshots
  5. Render PDF via @react-pdf/renderer
  6. Upload PDF to Cloudflare R2
  7. Email customer via Resend (with PDF attachment)
  8. Award gems (₹100 = 1 gem, floor)
  9. Fire Meta CAPI Purchase event (if lead-attributed)

Email Delivery & Failure Handling
  • Email sent via Resend with PDF attachment from R2 buffer
  • Template: invoice-receipt.tsx (React Email)
  • Failure → retry via QStash (max 3 attempts, exponential backoff)
  • After 3 failures → log error, invoice still accessible in admin
  • PDF always available via R2 regardless of email delivery status
