No-Show Consequences — Tier System

1-3

Tier 1–3: Tag Only

CRM tag applied for internal tracking. No penalty applied to the customer. Staff can see the count.

4+

Tier 4+: Manager Approval Required

Future bookings require explicit manager approval before confirmation. Customer is notified of the restriction.

!

Escalation Path

Repeated no-shows (4+) trigger review. Manager decides: approve, deny, or require deposit (future feature).

✓

Reset: 3 Consecutive Completes

3 consecutive completed bookings clears the no-show flag. Customer returns to normal booking flow.
